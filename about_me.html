About me

Three passions, simple but overwhelmingly strong, have governed my life: the longing for love, the search for knowledge, and unbearable pity for the suffering of mankind.

— Bertrand Russell

Hi! I am Haoran Liang (梁浩然), a second-year Master ’s student at ETH Zurich. Previously, I did my Bachelor ’s degree at Xi ’an Jiaotong University. I am a big number theory fan with broad interests in its intersecting areas. For instance, these following topics are quite appealing to me

Automorphic forms and representations;

Rational points on varieties;

Cohomological topics in number theory;

p-adic analogues and p-adic methods.

Outside mathematics, I love reading, playing badminton, listening to music, and visiting various kinds of museums, etc. Before the triumph of AI, I enjoyed playing Go very much (seriously).

Please feel free to reach me if you find anything interesting at: hrliang dot maths at gmail dot com
